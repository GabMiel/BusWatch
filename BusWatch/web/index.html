Features for Parents

Interactive Map

Monitor your child's bus live.

Arrival Estimates

Never miss a stop. View estimated arrival times (ETA) for your bus stop.

Boarding Status

Get instant updates when your child boards or deboards the bus. Monitor status changes in real-time for both morning and afternoon routes.

Trip Monitoring

Ensuring safety beyond tracking. Administrators have instant access to student medical profiles and emergency contacts during active trips.

SOS Alerts

A secure platform where drivers / conductors can send instant SOS signals to school administrators during critical incidents for rapid response.

Verified Network

Only verified parents, drivers, and conductors can access the system, ensuring student data is kept private and secure.
